Themes
Women Safety
Safety and security invasions are an everyday phenomenon for women in India. Women traveling alone often encounter threats to safety like harassment, kidnapping and heinous crime rape. The inadequacy of safety is one of the biggest reasons for falling employment rates among women in India. Our hackathon endeavours to empower women of our country through innovative and implementable ideas that can create and promote the safety of women.
Open Innovation
Open innovation is a platform for all those astonishing agile brains out there who have fabulous ideas to start a new business using any technology or concept they prefer. It is the perfect opportunity for developers to showcase their creativity as they are not bound to any particular problem or technology. Our hackathon encourages the development of creative solutions for any critical problem.
AR/VR
Augmented/Virtual reality is one of the most promising technologies of the near future. Pokemon Go and Snapchat lenses are the perfect examples of the potential of AR. Using VR devices one can get comprehensive compelling experience shutting out the physical world. Our hackathons encourage the AR/VR designers to innovate and create any inspiring product or provide a solution for any problem using AR/VR.
Blockchain
Blockchain makes the history of any digital asset unchangeable and transparent through the use of decentralisation and cryptographic hashing. The peer-to-peer nature of blockchain eliminates the requirement for a centralised platform resulting in lower transaction fees and enhanced data transparency. The focus of our hackathon is to build a decentralised application to share assets among the individuals.

FAQs

How big can my team be?

The maximum team size allowed is 4.

When?

HackVSIT 2020 will be a 24 hours hackathon which will stretch from 31st January to 01st February 2020.

Will there be food arrangement?

What are the criteria of short listing the teams?

The registered participants will be shortlisted on the basis of their GitHub and LinkedIn profiles.

What is the eligibility criteria?

You must be a student from any University/College or Higher Secondary School in India.

Can I register individually?

Yes, but you are required to make a team for the event beforehand.

Where?

HackVSIT is at Vivekananda Institute of Professional Studies AU- Block (Outer Ring Road), Pitampura, Delhi - 110034.

How much does it cost?

Zero. Zip. Zilch. Nada. Nothing. Admission to HackVSIT is completely free, thanks to our sponsors.

What do I bring with me to the event?

You are required to bring your school/college ID, laptops, phone, chargers. A pillow and sleeping bag may be useful as well.

Do you provide Travel reimbursement?

Top 10 teams (from outside delhi) will be reimbursed a certain amount. We'll keep you posted on our social media handles. Stay tuned!
